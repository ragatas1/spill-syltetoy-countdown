It's a cold monday morning. You have been excited all winter for the friend meet up in Lindesnes, paradise in the southern north. A break from the snow and constant darkness in your daily life. Unfortunetely, your bus for train has taken a stop in the place the sun never rises, Tromsø. Due to the blackout, the only thing you see, are snow and grey buildings. Winter depression chills up your spine, as your phone dies with a sad vibration. The roar from the steam pipe from the train in the station echoes in your mind. Your train ticket is in your phone. The digital sign glows like a feline eye. 23 pm, is when your train leaves for the endless spring days you have waited for. There must be one weirdo in this town that has the fabled power bank the local Elkjøp keeps talking about. 

Your turn around. In your view, two buildings rising above the endless fog.
